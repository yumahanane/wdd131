Beira, Mozambique

Mozambique is located in the east coast of Southern Africa. It borders with Tanzania and Malawi in the north, Zambia and Zimbabwe in the west, and Swaziland and South Africa in the south. It is rich in natural resources such as natural gas, coal, minerals, and hydropower. Tourists can enjoy paradisiacal beaches, diverse gastronomy, and safaris.
