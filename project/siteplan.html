Site Plan

Overview

Name

The site is called, "Just a Yuman." The name comes from the book that the site gives the readers access to. The book is a journal of a little human called Yuma; or simply, it is a book about a Yuman. The readers will appreciate the name once they read the book's introduction."

Logo

Purpose

The site gives the readers access to the book, "Yuma's Journal - Dreaming." In the site, the readers are able to read the book and download it as a pdf. The site also provides information about the author, the reviews and feedback the book has previously received, it allows the readers to send their comments and reviews through a form, and to subscribe to the newsletter through a button.

Scenarios

The site will answer to the following questions:

  • Where can I obtain a copy of the book?
  • Where can I share my feedback about the book?
  • When will the next volume of the book be published?

Color Schema

The background color will be #D9F3FF and the navigation will be #FF9671

Typography

The body will be Opens Sans

For example: Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Totam ipsum unde tempora qui minus eligendi, quisquam natus dicta repellendus aliquam.

The navigation and headings will be Crimson Text

For example: Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Quo cupiditate officia atque natus, a magni tempora saepe eveniet dignissimos deserunt.
