Yuma's Journal
DREAMING

by Yuma Natalia Hanane

Introduction

I first named this Meu Livro (Portuguese for My Book). I have always been very lazy to write, but for the first time I was able to write something. It started as one page only. And I did not think it was going to go more than that. But I still liked the fact that I thought I could write a whole book. Then, I thought of the name Chronicles of Yuman (as in, stories of Yuma who is just a human).

The truth is that the name does not really matter for me. I still have not decided if I am going to keep this for my future children only or make it public. I have talked about a lot of serious and hurtful things here in a way that does not reflect how sad I truly feel. But I am new to expressing my thoughts and feelings. Not just in writing, just in general. Then, I can only hope for your empathy.

I give thanks to the author of Bird by Bird who has inspired me to write, and to my very good friend B who is my only fan.
